I’m a postdoc at the University of Southern California, working with Prof. Antonio Ortega in electrical engineering. I got my Bachelor’s from Yonsei University, guided by Prof. Dong-Hyun Kim, and my Master’s at KAIST with Prof. Joohwan Chun. I earned my Ph.D. at EPFL, Switzerland, studying under Prof. Nikolas Geroliminis from 2017 to 2022. While I am open to exploring any area of knowledge, my current primary research interests lie in graph signal processing, traffic flow prediction, radar signal processing, and deep neural networks.
