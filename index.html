Semin Kwak is currently studying for a Ph.D. at EPFL. He majored in electrical and electronic engineering and has conducted research in several fields. During his bachelor's degree, he conducted in-depth research on the sharpening of MRI images, while at KAIST he mainly studied radar signal processing. He is currently working on the prediction of graph signals in beautiful Switzerland.
