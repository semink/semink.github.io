I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Southern California, collaborating with Prof. Antonio Ortega and contributing to the ever-evolving realm of electrical engineering. I began my academic journey at Yonsei University in Seoul, Republic of Korea, where I earned my Bachelor's degree under the guidance of Prof. Dong-Hyun Kim. Furthering my studies, I completed my Master's at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), mentored by Prof. Joohwan Chun. Drawn to the esteemed corridors of the Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) in Switzerland, I pursued and achieved my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering. Throughout my doctoral studies from April 2017 to October 2022, I was under the expert tutelage of Prof. Nikolas Geroliminis.
